# Waivers

- Teams can release a player to the waiver wire and other teams can submit claims for the waived contract.
- If claimed, the team with the highest waiver priority will get the player and will pay 100% of the player's salary, while the original team will have no cap penalty for the waiver.
- If a player is not claimed by another GM within 48 hours, the original team will be assessed a cap penalty worth 50% of the waived contract and the player will become an UFA.
- A team can choose to absorb the entire penalty in the current year or stretch it out over the length of the original contract. For example, if a waived player has a contract worth $5 million this year and $5 million dollars next year, the GM can choose to take the entire $5 million ($10M*.5) cap penalty in the current year, or receive a cap penalty of $2.5 million this year and $2.5 million next year. 
* To absorb the entire penalty in the current year you must make it clear that you intend to do so in your waiver post *
- If a player clears waivers without being claimed, they will become an Unrestricted Free Agent.
- The team that waived the player cannot sign the same player for the remainder of the season.
- Each season's starting waiver priority is the reverse order of the team standings from the previous season
- To claim a player off waivers within the 48 hour claim window, reply to the thread created for that player stating that you claim that player for your team. At the end of the 48 hours the team with the highest waiver priority will win that claim.
- After you win a waived player, you are placed at the bottom of the waiver priority.
- When dropping a player, post it to the "Waivers" sub-board as designated. The title should be the player's name and their contract information should be included in the post.

# Retiring Players

- If a player retires, you will be forced to cut the player and will be penalized 50% of the remaining contract either in one year or spread out over the length of the contract. You can hang onto them until they announce retirement but once they do, they must be cut and can't be used as a 0 fpts roster spot for a tanking team.

# Conditional Drops

- Allowed for trading purposes or bidding on RFA's and UFA's.
- In your post you must specify which player you plan to drop if your trade is passed or bid is won.
- After your trade has passed or bid is won, you must make a post in the "Waivers" thread.

# Amnesty

- Each GM has a one time amnesty clause that can be used for any player.
- You will only be allowed to have one for your entire stay in Deep Three Dynasty, so use it wisely.
- Post your amnesties in the "Amnesty" sub-board. The title should be the player's name and their contract information should be included in the post.
- If a team amnesties a player, they will not be allowed to re-sign the player immediately following the amnesty unless an entire offseason has passed.
- An amnestied player's contract will be treated as a "free drop" with no cap hit and the player will be released directly to UFA. 
- Amnestied players are not eligible to be claimed on waivers. 
- Amnesty clauses are not tradable.
