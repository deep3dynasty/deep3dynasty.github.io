# Draft Lottery

- The lottery will be based on the NBA Draft lottery.
- Pre-lottery standings will be determined by regular season record.
- Each D3D team will take the place of the team in the NBA that correlates with their place in the standings and we will follow the results of the NBA Draft Lottery.
- All record-based tie breakers will be determined by total fantasy points scored.  

- Example:
- In real life the Charlotte Hornets have the #1 pick odds. Prior to the lottery, Team A in D3D with the worst record owns the same pick odds as that #1 seeded IRL Hornets team.
- IRL Charlotte Hornets pick falls down to #4 in the actual draft, therefore Team A in D3D will move down to #4. This same concepts applies to all teams in the lottery, and they will follow their IRL team's seeding. 

# Draft

- The draft for our league will be held on Fantrax.
- Each team's draft clock is 12 hours for both the 1st round and the 2nd round.
- If you fail to make your pick within the time limit, the next team can make their pick. The draft will continue without giving you another window, so get your pick in as soon as possible if you miss it.
- You are allowed to trade even if you are not on the clock.
- Please note that you can only draft players who are eligible and declare themselves for that year's draft class.
