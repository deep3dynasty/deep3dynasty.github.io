# Team Options

- A team can only sign only one UFA contract or Re-sign contact with a Team Option per offseason. (Either one UFA contract or re-sign contract, not both) And, you can only have one leading bid in UFA with a TO at any time. Having multiple leading bids with TO's will cause all of your leading bids to be invalid.
- A team can offer only one bid on a RFA with a Team Option per offseason. Submitting multiple bids with TO's will cause ALL of your team's bid to be invalid. This offer does not affect a team's ability to bid and sign a player with a TO in UFA. If the leading bid on a RFA has a TO and is either won by the bidder or matched by the rights holding team, neither team loses their ability to sign an UFA to a contract with a TO. 
- If, by the deadline (during offseason prior to UFA), a team does not explicitly state that they will be accepting a player's Team Option for next season, that player's option will automatically be accepted, as long as the team has the available roster and cap space.
- Team options cannot be accepted without available cap room.
- Team option must be accepted before the player is involved in a trade. As a reminder, you have to have the available cap to accept the option on your salary cap before trading the player.
- Team options declined on rookie deal players during the offseason will send the player directly to UFA.
- In the case of a team denying a qualifying offer (QO) on rookie deals (i.e. like denying their option), teams will not be allowed to sign players that had those declined QOs in UFA until after the first game of the season has been played. Those players are also ineligible for a re-sign tag.

# Re-sign Tags

- Every team will be given 2 re-sign tags to use within 3 years. After 3 years, 2 new tags are granted. Please consult the spreadsheet for the reset date. These tags do not roll over. This tag can be used to extend a player once their contract expires.
- Re-sign tags are available to all players provided they meet criteria below.
- A re-signed player must have at minimum 2 seasons of at least 30 GP in order to be re-signed.
- To determine their contract value, calculate the average FPPG (Fantasy points per game) for the most recent 2 seasons the player has 30 GP or more. (FPPG can be found on fantrax by navigating to the player's profile page and selecting the "stats" tab)
- If a player failed to play 30 games the season prior, their contract will be based on the most recent season they have met this requirement.
*We will be lowering the threshold to 24 games for the shortened 2019-2020 season, and 26 games for the 2020-2021 season.
- If a player has never played at least 30 games in a season, throughout their career, for at least 2 seasons they are not eligible to receive a Re-sign Tag.
- The minimum contract length for a re-sign is 2 years.
- The maximum contract length for a re-sign is 4 years.
- You can apply the 1 team option per offseason to a re-sign contract. If a team option is used, the minimum contract length for the re-sign must be 3 seasons (2 seasons + 1 year team option.)
- Resign tag contract values will be based on a percentage of the salary cap. They are below:

0-9.99 FPPG = 2.5% of the salary cap
10-14.99 FPPG = 5% of the salary cap
15-19.99 FPPG = 10% of the salary cap
20-24.99 FPPG = 15% of the salary cap
25-29.99 FPPG = 17.5% of the salary cap
30-34.99 FPPG = 20% of the salary cap
35-39.99 FPPG = 22.5% of the salary cap
40-44.99 FPPG = 25% of the salary cap
45-49.99 FPPG = 27.5% of the salary cap
50-54.99 FPPG = 30% of the salary cap
55+ FPPG = 32.5% of the salary cap.

- To re-sign a player, create a post with the player's name in the "Re-signs" section of Free Agency. In the post state the player's name, show their FPPG average meeting the above requirements, state the number of years you wish to sign the player for, and calculate OCV.

* For example: Re-signing Victor Oladipo before the 2021-2022 season.

2020-2021 season - 33 GP (meets requirement) - 42.3 FPPG
2019-2020 season - 19 GP (does NOT meet requirement) - SKIP
2018-2019 season - 36 GP (meets requirement) - 42.5 FPPG

(42.3 + 42.5) FPPG / 2 = 42.4 FPPG average (25% of salary cap)
Salary Cap = $118M - so - $118M * 0.25 = $29.5M

* Example of Re-sign post:
Post Title: Victor Oladipo
The Georgia Tech Yellowjackets re-sign Victor Oladipo
42.3 FPPG + 42.5 FPPG / 2 = 42.4 FPPG
$29.5M for 3 years - OCV = $29.5M x 1.8 = 53.1 OCV

# Rookie Contracts

- Rookie contracts will follow the real NBA contract for that player based on Spotrac.
- Non-guaranteed years on IRL contracts will be treated as Team Options. Partially guaranteed will count as a fully contracted year.
- Teams do not need to sign their rookies immediately upon drafting them. A team has until the start of the regular season to sign their rookies. A player not signed will be waived before the start of the season with no penalty and become an UFA. The GM will lose their draft rights to that player.
- A team cannot sign a rookie if it puts them over the cap.
- Two-way contracts will be listed in the spreadsheet as $300,000 for 3 seasons with Team Options in seasons 2 and 3. If during the season they are signed for the rest of the season by their IRL team, then their cap hit will increase to $1M for that season. 
- Players drafted who do not sign an NBA contract immediately can be signed by their drafted team for $300,000 for 3 seasons (with the 3rd season being a Team Option) before they become UFA's.
- If an undrafted or 2way player signs an NBA contract at a later date during their current contract, the team will have the option to take on that IRL contract value OR release the player to UFA.
- If a two-way contract ends while a player is still on your roster and has not been signed to a standard NBA deal, they become an UFA. 
- When players are drafted but do not get an IRL contract right away, these players must be on the G-League roster until they receive their IRL contract. For example, Chuma Okeke was drafted in 2019 but does not get a contract until 2020 and thus cannot be put on the active roster until the contract is given.
