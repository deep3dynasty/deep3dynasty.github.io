# Scoring

Points (PTS): 1
Rebounds (REB): 1.25
Assists (AST): 1.5
Steals (STL) : 2
Blocks (BLK): 2
3-Pointers Made (3PTM): 1
Turnovers (TO): -1
Field Goals Percentage (FG%): 1
Free Throws Percentage (FT%): 1

# Schedule

- There will be 18 total regular season matchups per season. 
- The schedule is a rotating 4 season schedule. During that 4 season rotating schedule, each team will play their in-division opponents 5 times (at least once per division rival per season), each intra-conference opponent 3 times (same conference), and each inter-conference opponent 2 times (other conference).
- Depending on the IRL NBA schedule (lockouts/suspensions/etc), there may be alterations to the schedule as needed.

# Playoffs

- Playoffs will begin week 19 (in a standard schedule NBA season).
- Each conference will send the top 8 teams to the playoffs. After the NBA changed its rules, we no longer guarantee division winners a playoff berth.
- Teams will be seeded based on overall record.
- Tiebreakers for playoff spots and seeding will be:
1. Head-to-Head Record for current season (if one exists)
2. Total Fantasy Points
- Each playoff matchup in the first 3 rounds will be 1 week long, with the Finals being 2 weeks long. The winner of this final matchup will be crowned the Champion of the League.


# Playoff Rosters

- Rosters lock for non-playoff teams at the end of the regular season. No trades and no additions can happen for non-playoff teams after the start of the playoffs. (Exceptions will be made for players who's bidding was started before midnight EST of the night before the playoffs start.)
- Playoff teams are allowed to add or drop players, but the trade deadline will still be in effect. No trades during the playoffs.
- Non playoff teams are allowed to drop players, but those players are not eligible to be added by playoff teams. 
- Any player signed during the playoffs can only be signed for the remainder of the season. Multi-year deals are ineligible. Normal UFA bidding rules still apply. These players will not be eligible for re-signs/extensions.
- Cuts made by playoff teams are still regular cuts with cap penalties. If you cut someone with multiple years left, you either take the full penalty this season, or stretch it out over the remaining years on the contract. The players will join the UFA pool for the offseason. There is no waivers system in the playoffs.
