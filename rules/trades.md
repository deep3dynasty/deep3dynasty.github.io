# Trade Council

- The Trade Council (TC) consists of 7 GMs who vote on trades, either to approve or to veto.
- A trade is deemed approved or vetoed once it receives 3 approvals or vetoes.
- Once a trade receives 3 approvals or vetoes, the thread ends there. No more Trade Committee members need comment. As soon as a trade receives its 3rd approval, the moderators may update the spreadsheet to reflect the changes. However, changes to your Fantrax roster will not be made until the following day if the rosters have already locked on Fantrax.

# Trade Post Format

- Post the complete contract information for every player involved.
- Place each player and pick being traded for each team on separate lines, separated by team so the TC can clearly see who and what's being exchanged in the deal.
- Every time a pick is involved, please specify it in full, Year, Round & Team, even if the pick is being traded during the draft it is in.
- Both teams must post their acceptance and an explanation on the motivation behind the trade before it will be voted on by TC.
- Once both teams post their acceptance of the trade it won’t be possible to back out. So make sure to review your trades carefully.
- Your trade will not be voted on if it is not formatted the correct way.

# Tradable Assets

- All Players
- Rights to drafted rookies
- Rights to RFA (in the offseason they are entering RFA only)
- Draft Picks - *only picks 2 drafts out can be traded. Picks in the 2nd draft away from current offseason draft can be traded beginning at current season's check-in.
- Salary cap in the form of Salary Coverage of a rostered player. (Cash not attached to salary can not be traded.)

# Salary Coverage
- Teams are able to send out or receive up to 20% of the salary cap each season in salary coverage in trades for up to 2 years of a player's contract. In negotiations, a team can send or receive any percentage up to 25% of a player's salary involved in a deal. 

Example: Team A trades Vince Carter and his $20M contact to Team B, and Team A agrees to cover 25% of Vince Carter's salary for 1 season. That means Team A would keep $5M on their cap and Team B would receive a -$5M adjustment to their salary cap that season. 

- Trading is open from the official start of the offseason (check-in begins) until D3D’s Trade Deadline.
- No trade that will put any team over their cap limit will be allowed. In the event a team starts above the cap during the offseason, they are prohibited from making any trade that would add more to their cap. They must be under the cap by the start of the regular season
- In the event that a player is traded in their team option year before the team option decision period begins and there is salary coverage on that player's salary as a condition of that trade, this automatically triggers a picking up of the option by the team trading that player. This applies to every team option season where salary is covered as a condition of the agreed upon trade.
